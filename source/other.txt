Other free link checkers
========================

All of the programs below have an `Open Source license`_ like LinkChecker.
Programs without an Open Source license are not mentioned here for
security and copyright reasons.

.. _`Open Source license`:
   http://www.opensource.org/licenses/

Also not mentioned are programs which have not been updated for more than 10 years.


- `gURLChecker`_ written in C, last updated February 22, 2011 (version 0.13.1)
  
  .. _gURLChecker:
     http://labs.libre-entreprise.org/projects/gurlchecker/

- `W3C Link Checker`_ is an online service, but source is available, last updated 2011

  .. _W3C Link Checker:
     http://validator.w3.org/checklink/

- `webcheck`_ written in Python, last updated September 11, 2010 (version 1.10.4)
  
  .. _webcheck:
     http://arthurdejong.org/webcheck/

- `webgrep`_ written in Perl, last updated April 2004 (version 2.12)
  
  .. _webgrep:
     http://cgi.linuxfocus.org/~guido/index.html#webgrep
